# Role Expectations

Used by the agents as grounding context. Keyed by role title.

## Senior Frontend Engineer
- Reliable, high-quality delivery of frontend features
- Ownership of components and shared UI systems
- Mentoring of less experienced engineers
- Strong technical judgment and trade-off reasoning
- Cross-team collaboration with backend and design

## Middle Backend Engineer
- Reliable delivery of backend services and APIs
- Continuous learning and growth
- Collaboration across the team
- Code quality and test coverage

## QA Engineer
- Ownership of product quality and release gates
- Risk detection and prioritization
- Test strategy and automation
- Cross-functional collaboration with engineering and product

## Platform Engineer
- Reliability and availability of shared infrastructure
- Ownership of CI/CD and developer tooling
- Automation that reduces toil for other teams
- Cross-team enablement and support

## Engineering Manager
- Team delivery, health, and growth
- Fair, evidence-grounded performance feedback
- Clear goals and prioritization
- Cross-team alignment
