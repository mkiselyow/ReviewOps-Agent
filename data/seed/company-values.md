# Company Values

These are the company values ReviewOps Agent maps evidence against.

## Own It
Take end-to-end responsibility for outcomes, not just tasks. Follow through on
commitments and proactively surface and resolve problems.

## Act with Speed
Make well-reasoned decisions quickly, ship in small increments, and keep
momentum without sacrificing quality.

## Challenge Convention
Question the status quo constructively. Propose better approaches and back them
with evidence.

## Work in the Grey
Make progress under ambiguity. Move forward with incomplete information while
managing risk responsibly.

## Stand Out by Design
Care about craft and quality. Deliver work that is thoughtful, polished, and
raises the bar for the team.
